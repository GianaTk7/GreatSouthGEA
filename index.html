Facts About Us

Fact 1

Great South Autobody is a family-owned business that has been operating for over 30 years. We are a proud member of the Retail Motor Industry Organisation (RMI) and the Motor Industry Workshop Association (MIWA).

Fact 2

Our team of highly skilled technicians are trained to work on all makes and models of vehicles. We use the latest diagnostic equipment and tools to ensure that your vehicle is repaired to the highest standard.

Fact 3

Great South Autobody offers a wide range of services, including panel beating, spray painting, mechanical repairs, and auto electrical repairs. We also offer a 24-hour towing service for your convenience.
